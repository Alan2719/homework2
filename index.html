About Me

Hi, my name is Alan. I was born in Mexico City. I'm the middle brother. I studied the major in automotive design engineering at Tec de Monterrey. I consider myself a person who always achieves my goals and learn from my mistakes. Right now, I work at Ford Motor Company in the testing area. Before working at Ford, I was working at Sulzer. It is a company that designs hydraulic pumps. I was in the design area. After that, I moved to the sales area. My main task was to quote the total price of a pump for all the subsidiaries around the world. Right now, at Ford my team and I are designing a new system for Product Development department.


I got interested in coding while I was studying my major. I learned to code in C, Python, Matlab and a little bit of C#. With the C language, I learned to program an Arduino to work with electronic components. I used Matlab to code finite element method which is used a lot in the automotive industry. Since then, I have been learning on my own in other languages like C++.

Right now, I'm trying to improve my skills in the coding area which I like a lot because you are able to create big things by starting from a simple idea. My goal is to create new things and innovate in this industry, also keep learning because coding can be applied in almost every industry.
