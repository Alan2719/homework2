About Me

Hi, my name is Alan. I was born in Mexico City. I have 2 brothers, one older and one younger. When I was 6 years old I moved to the state of Mexico and I've been living here for almost 20 years. Since I was a child I have been interested in learning new things and I was very curious to understand how things work. My favorite sport is soccer. I'm a big fan of Futbol Club Barcelona. Another one of my favorite sports is Formula 1. I have always been interested in mechanics and especially in the automotive industry.
When I grew up I decided to study the major in automotive design engineering at Tec de Monterrey. During my major, I learned to code in several languages like C++, C#, python, and Matlab. That's when I started to like coding.

My favorite hobbies are to travel and discover new places, hang out with my friends every week, listening to music, especially electronic music, coding although I want to take it further, playing and watching soccer, trying new food, reading, and learning new things in general. Right now, I work at Ford Motor Company in the testing area. My goal in this coding program is to learn all I can, get experience in the coding industry and have my own business.
